\# Business KPI Dashboard | Power BI



\## Overview

This project presents an interactive Business KPI Dashboard developed using Power BI on the Olist E-commerce dataset (Brazil). The dashboard provides a consolidated view of key business metrics, enabling analysis of sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.



\## Objectives

\- Monitor and track key business performance indicators

\- Analyze revenue trends over time

\- Identify top-performing products and categories

\- Evaluate customer contribution to revenue

\- Assess payment behavior and delivery performance



\## Key KPIs

\- Total Revenue: 16M+

\- Total Orders: 99K+

\- Total Customers: 96K+

\- Average Order Value: 160.99

\- Average Delivery Time: 12 Days



\## Dashboard Features



\### Revenue Analysis

\- Revenue Trend Over Time

\- Revenue by Product Category

\- Top Products by Revenue



\### Customer Analysis

\- Top Customers by Revenue



\### Operational Analysis

\- Delivery Time Distribution

\- Revenue by Payment Method



\### Interactivity

\- Slicers for dynamic filtering

\- Interactive visual exploration



\## Tools and Technologies

\- Power BI (Data Visualization and Dashboard Development)

\- DAX (KPI Calculations and Measures)

\- Power Query (Data Cleaning and Transformation)



\## Data Modeling

\- Integrated multiple datasets including orders, customers, products, and payments

\- Established relationships to enable cross-table analysis

\- Created calculated measures for KPI tracking and analysis



\## Key Insights

\- A small group of products and categories contributes significantly to total revenue

\- Revenue trends indicate variation over time, suggesting potential seasonality

\- A limited number of customers account for a high share of revenue

\- Payment methods are concentrated across a few dominant types

\- Delivery time distribution highlights variation in operational performance
